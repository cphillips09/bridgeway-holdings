What is Private Credit?

Private credit is non-bank lending to mostly private, middle-market companies that aren’t publicly traded or issued. It is one of the fastest-growing segments in the lending landscape, with close to $1.3 trillion in assets under management. Private credit’s rapid growth, coupled with its opacity and comparatively light regulation, place a premium on insight into current and evolving risks in specific sectors. With Fed policy intent on maintaining higher interest rates to fight persistent inflation, spreads are widening and the need for private credit is increasing.

Source: Moodys

Who is Bridgeway Holdings?

Bridgeway Holdings is a mezzanine debt provider focusing on deals between $2 - $10 million. We focus on:

  • Bridging to a liquidity event
  • AR, inventory & equipment financing
  • Providing real estate equity, primarily on industrial projects
  • Interim financing to help companies avoid giving away equity to secure needed financial assistance
